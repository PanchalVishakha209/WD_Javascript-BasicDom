Q-7. What is the difference between ViewState and SessionState?
ANS.The basic difference between these two is that 
the ViewState is to manage state at the client's end, making state management easy for end-user 
while SessionState manages state at the server's end, making it easy to manage content from this end too. 
ViewState: It is maintained at only one level that is page-level.

Q-8. What is === operator?
ANS.The strict equality ( === ) operator checks whether its two operands are equal, returning a Boolean result.
 Unlike the equality operator, the strict equality operator always considers operands of different types to be different.
